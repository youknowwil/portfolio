The Lil Things

E-commerce

  • Branding, Website
  • 2015-2017

An e-commerce website that sells small gifts and design & photography services for celebratory events. The Lil Things was created to help people lessen the stress when it came to organizing and planning a special event.

To simplify the process, the website allows shoppers to connect with various services in one place. They can purchase an array of small gifts (such as wedding favours), have them personalized to their liking (e.g., laser etching), get custom designs (e.g., card invitations), and on-site photography.
