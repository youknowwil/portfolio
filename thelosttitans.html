The Lost Titans

Browser MMORPG

  • Content, Digital, Website
  • 2012-2014

Is a free-to-play browser-based MMORPG, set in the fantasy world of Aristos. Developed by ZQGames and published globally by GBE Games Ltd.

The Lost Titans is a fully 3D-rendered game, played directly in any modern web browser (no-installation needed). Players will find a full host of traditional MMORPG features, including solo & group Faction PvP, pet training and combat, and a large-scale party system for completing world events.
