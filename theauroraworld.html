The Aurora World

MMORPG

  • Content, Digital, Identity, Print, Website
  • 2013-2015

Is a free-to-play 3D fantasy MMORPG with a heavy Eastern-influence style. The Aurora World features four playable classes (each having two distinct professions) and a unique open-world PvE system that also includes instance dungeons.

Developed by Aurora Interactive and published globally by GBE Games Ltd.
