About & Contact

Hi,

I’m a Toronto-born (Canada) creative designer who helps businesses craft and develop their brand through digital communication.

In the last 10 years, I've worked as a designer and art director at GBE Games Ltd. & Game Bridger Entertainment. There I helped design, strategize, and market globally the launch of numerous digital products in the free-to-play video game publishing industry.

After leaving in 2015 to pursue my own aspirations, a friend and I launched an e-commerce and design service company called, The Lil Things — an online store & studio that catered to weddings and other celebratory events.

Currently freelancing full-time while working on my own mobile app.
