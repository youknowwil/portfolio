Priston Tale 2:
The 2nd Enigma

MMORPG

  • Digital, Print
  • 2011-2015

Is a free-to-play 3D fantasy MMORPG and the sequel to Priston Tale (2001). Fans of the original will recognize some of the familiar elements carried over, but outside of that, it's been entirely rebuilt using the Unreal Engine (rare at the time of development). Along with the new visuals and revamped gameplay, Priston Tale 2 introduces two news races: Rogue and Pilgrim.

Developed by YD Online & published in Europe and South America by GamerKraft/Game Bridger Entertainment.
