FreeJack

Racing

  • Digital
  • 2010-2013

Is a free-to-play 3D multiplayer online (MO) racing game based on the concept of "freerunning" (a way of expression by interacting with various obstacles and environment1). Players assume the role of one of the initial four playable characters and partake in footraces (either against the AI or other players), where they traverse through the many colourful urban cityscapes of New Jack City!
Watch some of the videos.

Developed by WiseOn and published globally by Gamerkraft/Game Bridger Entertainment.

FreeJack went through two iterations, the latter being a revamp of the gameplay mechanics and a slight rebrand.
