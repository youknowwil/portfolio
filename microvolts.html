MicroVolts

MMO TPS

  • Print
  • 2012

Is a free-to-play MMO third-person-shooter game that features a fun cast of colourful characters, a wide array of player customizations, and a Team Fortress 2-inspired gameplay. Battle it out across dozens of large well-designed maps and frag your enemies with any of the 7 different weapon types.

Developed by NQGames and published globally by Rock Hippo Productions.
