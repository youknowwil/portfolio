Hero of the Obelisk

MMORPG

  • Content, Digital, Website
  • 2013-2015

Formerly known as Dungeon Hero, Hero of the Obelisk is a free-to-play dungeon-crawl MMORPG developed by Eyasoft and published globally by GBE Games Ltd.

HOTO features a hack n’ slash combat system in a world offering open-world PvP and a variety of challenging instance dungeons that players can tackle solo or in a party.
