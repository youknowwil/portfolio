Blade 9

MMORPG

  • Content, Digital, Website
  • 2013-2015

Developed by MoreFun Online and published globally by GBE Games Ltd.

Blade 9 is a free-to-play fantasy MMORPG (massive multiplayer online role playing game). Players embark on a quest to defeat evil by finding and reassembling a legendary divine weapon. Once whole, it shattered into multiple shards and assumed the form of 9 weapons, known as Ruins.
